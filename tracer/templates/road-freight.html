FAQ

What is FTL vs LTL?

FTL = full truck load for exclusive use. LTL = shared truck space for smaller shipments.

How are transit times calculated?

Transit includes driving time; allow for border delays and loading/unloading.

Do you handle international borders?

Yes — we manage customs and permits for cross-border transport.
