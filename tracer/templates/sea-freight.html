FAQ

What is FCL vs LCL?

FCL = full container load. LCL = consolidation of multiple shippers in one container.

How are transit times calculated?

Transit includes sea voyage only; allow additional days for port handling and customs clearance.

Do you provide inland transport?

Yes — we can arrange truck pickup and delivery to/from port.
