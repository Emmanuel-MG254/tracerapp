FAQ

What is express vs standard air freight?

Express offers faster delivery with higher priority. Standard is cost-effective for non-urgent cargo.

How are transit times calculated?

Transit includes flight time only; allow additional time for airport handling and customs.

Do you provide ground transport?

Yes — we can arrange pickup and delivery to/from airports.
